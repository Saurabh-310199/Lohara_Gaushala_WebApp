    Our History

    Lohara Gaushala has a long-standing tradition of love and respect for cows. Founded with the blessings of villagers and social workers, the Gaushala began as a small shelter for a few abandoned cows. Over the years, it has grown into a well-managed organization, providing food, clean water, medical treatment, and a natural living environment for hundreds of cows.

    The organization not only focuses on cow protection but also works towards creating awareness about the importance of cows in Indian culture and sustainable farming. With the support of kind-hearted donors and volunteers, Lohara Gaushala continues its journey of compassion, service, and preservation of Indian traditions.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to protect and serve cows with love and compassion. We aim to provide food, shelter, and medical care to every cow in need and preserve the cultural values of cow protection.
