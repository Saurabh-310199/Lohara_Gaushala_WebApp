About Us

Lohara Gaushala has a long-standing tradition of love and respect for cows. Founded with the blessings of villagers and social workers, the Gaushala began as a small shelter for a few abandoned cows.

Over the years, it has grown into a well-managed organization, providing food, clean water, medical treatment, and a natural living environment for hundreds of cows.
