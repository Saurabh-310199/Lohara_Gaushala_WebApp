    Who Are We?

    Lohara Gaushala is a humble shelter dedicated to the protection and care of cows. Our mission is to provide food, medical support, and a safe environment for abandoned, injured, and needy cows. With the blessings of our community and the generous support of donors, we continue to serve the holy cows with love and devotion.

    Our vision is to preserve Indian tradition, protect Gau Mata, and inspire people to join this noble cause.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to protect and serve cows with love and compassion. We aim to provide food, shelter, and medical care to every cow in need and preserve the cultural values of cow protection.

    About Us

    Lohara Gaushala has a long-standing tradition of love and respect for cows. Founded with the blessings of villagers and social workers, the Gaushala began as a small shelter for a few abandoned cows.

    Over the years, it has grown into a well-managed organization, providing food, clean water, medical treatment, and a natural living environment for hundreds of cows.
